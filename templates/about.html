About This Project

This project is designed to process and analyze EEG data in real-time, eventually utilizing machine learning techniques to identify meaningful patterns. It serves as a foundation for future advancements in neurological research and mental health assessment.

Future Improvements

Future iterations will incorporate a deep learning model capable of remotely analyzing EEG data to assess the likelihood of depression. This will provide users with an accessible, AI-driven tool for preliminary mental health insights. By leveraging neural networks and real-time data streaming, the system will enhance early detection and analysis, supporting both researchers and individuals.
